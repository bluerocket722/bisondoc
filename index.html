Why organizations are leaving Microsoft Office

It isn't just about price. In 2025 the EU forced Microsoft to unbundle Teams from Office after an antitrust case, and governments started walking away entirely: Denmark's Digitalisation Ministry — along with the cities of Copenhagen and Aarhus — began replacing Microsoft 365 with open formats, and the German state of Schleswig-Holstein is moving roughly 30,000 workstations off Microsoft to cut costs and keep control of their own data.

Bison Docs runs on those same open .docx and .xlsx standards — so you keep working in the formats every agency and client expects, while dropping the subscription. The same move, available to you today, for free.
